FGO Event Comp NP Damage Calculator (Beta v1.00)

Introduction

Welcome to the FGO Event Party Comp Damage Calculator! This calculator will allow you to enter enemy node waves, servant information, team comps, and event CE information to calculate efficient team compositions. This calculator will specify required NP levels, mystic codes, skill orders, and CE requirments for your desired event node.

Disclaimers

  • All servants are assumed to have NP charge, buff, and enemy debuff skills at rank 10
  • All servants available are assumed to have max atk foes (not including golden foes)
  • All servants available have either single target or AOE damage NPs
  • Any skill or NP upgrades available on the NA server are applied
  • Grails levels will not be calculated
  • Face-card damage and crits will not be taken into account
  • Buffs and debuffs will not be taken into account if success rate is not 100%
